> Utopia and dystopia are the same elephant

I certainly agree. Dystopia is how the future looks when your car broke down on the interstate in the pouring rain that morning and your coffee is perpetually cold, because in a certain sense (in all depictions of it in fiction), it is viscerally that forever.

Dystopias aren’t a thing that happens, anymore than tsundere is a thing that happens. It’s a fictional construct that can be applied to various degrees to describe an actual situation.
