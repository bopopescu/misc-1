On Writing Discordian Lit

We Discordians are all holy as fuck, and we have a direct line to Eris (dunno if she actually listens to these voice mails though).

That being said, there’s a lot of Discordian works that are essentially self-help books written by people who shouldn’t be giving advice to anybody.

There’s also a lot of hmmm, let’s say “low-effort” discordian lit,where somebody sat down and mashed the keyboard for 15 minutes, copy and pasted a bunch of kopyleft material they found, and said TA DAAA.

My advice to people adding to the Discordian library - think for yourself hard about it. Don’t just spout crazy sounding stuff in the spirit of Mal and Omar. Try to bring something new to the table. Write something that other people will find interesting, useful, challenging, whatever – but be original, take your time, and make a mark nobody’s made before.
