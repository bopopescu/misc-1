Clergy for the Strange

Discordians are the Clergy for the Strange. This is an important aspect of the Discordian Society which the world really needs right now.

There are a lot of people out there who don’t quite fit into this fucked up place. A lot of them are lonely or freaked out because they believe they are alone. Many don’t even know they’re weird yet, they just have this spiritual discomfort they haven’t learned to articulate.

I was privileged to grow up around open-minded free-thinkers, so I had a support network for my own bizarre interests and tastes. But I’m told it’s really alienating to be the one blue haired girl in a town full of cabbages. A lot of people have to keep their weirdness a secret, they bury it so deep they forget it’s there. It’s a terrible shame. Birds of paradise living in little gilded cages.

These people need our help. We’ve seen that you don’t have to play by the Rules, let’s show others that too. When I’m putting up weird posters around town, part of my motivation is as a signpost showing *somebody* out there that he’s not alone - other people are awake too, and when we all “find the others”, everything’s gonna turn out okay.

First, you’ve gotta escape your cell. Then you’ve gotta find the keys and pass them around. That is my best guess at how the Discordian Society can work together and accomplish something worth while … when we see somebody getting ground up by the Machine, we’ve gotta toss them a life jacket.

We are the priests and popes and cardinals and kings – of the strange. Throw open the doors of this church and invite everybody in.
